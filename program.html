About Program

The program endeavors to equip students with the necessary education and capabilities to find and assess potential business opportunities, and turn them into marketable and sustainable economic products and services and thus create new business and employment opportunities.

Aiming to increase the awareness of students in entrepreneurship, the program will not only present students with ample opportunities and facilities to gain and/or develop entrepreneurship skills but also will also teach them entrepreneurial behavior, attitude and capabilities. The Entrepreneurship programme is delivered by Ozyegin University.
