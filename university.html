University Overview

Özyegin University’s mission is to create, share, and apply knowledge in the service of society. Our applied research agenda creates useful knowledge. We share our knowledge through our educational programs which transform the lives of our students. Application of this knowledge generates economic benefits to the society through start-up and spin-out companies.

Top reasons to study here
  • It ‘s the third most popular foundation university in Turkey.
  • Özyegin University is among the top 10 universities in the Entrepreneurial and Innovative University Index of Turkey for the last three years consecutively.
  • It’s among the top 10 universities with 10 million TL of scientific research.
